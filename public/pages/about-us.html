About Meauxbility

Transforming pain into purpose through mobility, independence, and hope

Our Mission

Meauxbility is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to providing mobility grants and accessibility services to spinal cord injury survivors in Louisiana's Acadiana region. We believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to live independently and pursue their dreams, regardless of physical limitations.

Founded on the principles of hard work, willpower, inspiration, community, and faith, we transform obstacles into pathways for adaptive athletes and spinal cord injury survivors. Our mission is to ensure that mobility challenges never limit someone's potential to live a full, active life.

Our Story

Meauxbility was born from a deep understanding of the challenges faced by spinal cord injury survivors. Our founder, Sam Primeaux, experienced firsthand the barriers that exist when mobility is compromised. This personal journey became the foundation for creating an organization that not only provides financial support but also builds a community of hope and empowerment.

Since our founding, we have been committed to breaking down barriers and creating opportunities for those who refuse to let their circumstances define their future. Every grant we award, every resource we provide, and every connection we make is a step toward a more inclusive and accessible world.

Our Impact

Through our comprehensive programs and community support, we have:

  • Transformed 50+ lives through direct assistance and support
  • Awarded over $100,000 in mobility grants and equipment
  • Established partnerships with 15+ community organizations
  • Provided resources and support across 15 Louisiana parishes
  • Created a thriving community of adaptive athletes and advocates

Our Values

Hard Work

We believe in the power of determination and perseverance. Every challenge is an opportunity to grow stronger and more resilient.

Willpower

The strength to overcome obstacles comes from within. We support individuals in discovering and harnessing their inner power.

Inspiration
